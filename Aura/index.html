Why Aura Exists

Memory fades.
Care shouldn't.

People with psychological conditions often forget their history, lose critical files, and miss appointments — leaving both doctors and families in the dark at the worst possible moments.

The Problem

Patients with mental health conditions frequently lose medical records, forget diagnoses, and miss medication schedules — while their doctors are left without the full picture.

The Solution

Aura creates one shared, secure space — accessible by doctor, patient, and family — so the complete care history always exists, no matter what.
